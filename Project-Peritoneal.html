Overview

I am spearheading the development of an innovative Peritoneal Dialysis Simulator to provide practical training in ultrasound-guided procedures. After iterative attempts to create the best materials for ultrasound-guided functionality, we are now shifting the focus toward an IoT-based solution.This next phase will integrate electronics and programming to deliver real-time feedback, enhancing the learning experience for trainees. Our goal is to develop a cutting-edge, cost-effective simulator that not only meets clinical training needs but also leverages modern technology to improve procedural accuracy and patient safety.

Key Features:

  • Ultrasound-Guided Capability: Enables trainees to practice ultrasound-guided peritoneal dialysis procedures for enhanced accuracy and confidence.
  • IoT Integration (Future): Incorporates IoT-based technology for real-time feedback and data analytics to improve learning outcomes.
  • Realistic Task Trainer: Designed to replicate clinical scenarios with high fidelity to provide a hands-on, practical experience.
  • Cost-Effective Solution: Developed in-house to offer a budget-friendly alternative to existing international solutions without compromising quality.
  • Electronics and Programming Integration: Employs advanced electronics and programming for seamless operation and interactive features.
  • Customizable Design: Adjustable settings and components to meet diverse clinical training needs.
  • Scalable for Future Enhancements: Modular design to allow future upgrades, such as augmented reality (AR) or advanced sensor systems.
