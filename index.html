Projects

This section showcases innovative projects designed to meet the evolving needs of learners, faculty, and institutions. These solutions combine creativity, technical expertise, and a commitment to enhancing education, training, and practical applications across various domains.

VR-Based Intubation Task Trainer

A VR simulation designed to train healthcare professionals in performing fiberoptic intubation, utilizing Unity for development and Blender for anatomical modeling.

Heel Prick Simulator

A cost-effective, in-house simulator designed to train healthcare professionals in performing heel pricks on infants, enhancing practical skills and patient safety.

IntuBronch Box

The Intubronch Box offers a highly realistic, multi-layered airway simulation, designed to mirror true bronchus anatomy. Perfect for building confidence and skills in anesthesia residents and students.

Robo Receptionist

A smart robotic receptionist enhancing visitor engagement and navigation at CIME

Peritoneal Dialysis SImulator (In Progress)

Creating a task trainer for healthcare professionals to practice peritoneal dialysis. The trainer simulates the insertion of a catheter into the abdomen, allowing students to develop practical skills in a safe and controlled environment.
